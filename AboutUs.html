OUR STORY
Mong Kel Heng
Founder
In 2023, GS (GodStyle) was founded to empower people to reach higher levels of life. My unwavering belief ignited my passion for creating something truly remarkable. I set out to bring support to individuals on their journey to a stronger and more confident self. For that reason, top-notch quality, athletic design and cozy comfort are presented in every GS's products, including clothing, shoes and accessories.

GS will not just be a brand, but a trustworthy companion who travels with you in every step, offering you not just exceptional products but also unfaltering support, encouragement and inspiration to assist you in unleashing your full potential.
